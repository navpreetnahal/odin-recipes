Banana Pancake Recipe

A stack of bananas beautifully decorated with slices of bananas, syrup and melting

Ingredients

  • Flour
  • Sugar
  • Baking Powder
  • An Egg
  • Milk
  • Vegetable Oil
  • Bananas

Steps

  1. Combine dry ingredients like flour, sugar, salt, baking powder in one bowl and wet ingredients such as egg, milk, vegetable oil, mashed bananas in another bowl. Add the dry ingredients to the bowl with the wet ingredients, then stir until they're incorporated.
  2. Pour the batter in 1/4 cup portions onto a lightly oiled pan or griddle over medium-high heat. Cook for a few minutes, flip with a spatula, and cook for another few minutes or until each side is golden brown.
  3. Serve banana pancakes immediately. They are delicious alone or with your favorite pancake toppings.
